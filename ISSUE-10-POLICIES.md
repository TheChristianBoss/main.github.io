## Declared operating periods

- Job applications: up to 12 months after the role is closed, filled, or decided.
- General support records: up to 24 months after the last meaningful interaction.
- Order/accounting records: up to seven years when reasonably needed.
- Store fulfillment-idempotency records: approximately 120 days.
- Short job-portal rate-limit and duplicate records: minutes or hours.

The operator must follow the retention and response procedures in `POLICY-OPERATIONS.md` so the public statements remain accurate.
