Applicant Privacy

Job Applicant Privacy Notice

Effective July 18, 2026. This notice explains how Christian Goblin handles information submitted through its private job-code application portal.

Information collected

The application may request your full name, email address, preferred contact method, portfolio or examples link, availability, pay preference, relevant experience, answers to role-specific questions, and optional notes.

The application service may also process an application identifier, IP address, request timing, browser or device information, Cloudflare Turnstile results, rate-limit records, and duplicate-submission indicators.

How applications are used

  • review your qualifications and role-specific answers;
  • contact you about the application, an interview, follow-up questions, or another relevant role;
  • compare applicants and document hiring decisions;
  • prevent spam, automated submissions, duplicate applications, fraud, and abuse;
  • protect Christian Goblin’s systems and legal rights.

Submitting an application does not guarantee a response, interview, offer, contract, payment, or employment relationship.

Where the information goes

A Cloudflare Worker validates the application. Cloudflare Turnstile checks whether the submission appears to be made by a person rather than an automated system. Valid applications are forwarded to a private Discord channel used by the Christian Goblin hiring team.

Information may also be disclosed when reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to a valid legal request, investigate abuse, protect rights or safety, or obtain professional advice.

Christian Goblin does not sell applicant information.

Who can access applications

Access is intended to be limited to the Christian Goblin operator and people directly involved in reviewing or administering the relevant role. Discord and Cloudflare may process the information as service providers under their own policies.

Retention

Application content is retained for up to 12 months after the role is filled, closed, or a decision is made, unless a longer period is reasonably needed for a dispute, legal obligation, or documented business need. Short-term rate-limit and duplicate-submission records generally expire within minutes or hours.

Information may be deleted earlier when it is no longer needed or when a valid deletion request can be honored.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, withdrawal from consideration, or deletion by emailing hello@christiangoblin.com. Use the email address listed on the application when possible and identify the role.

Withdrawal stops future consideration but does not automatically require deletion of information that must reasonably be retained for security, dispute resolution, or legal obligations.

Applicant responsibilities

Submit truthful information that you have permission to share. Do not include passwords, authentication credentials, government IDs, financial-account information, sensitive medical information, or confidential information belonging to another person or employer.

Applicants under 18 should have permission from a parent or legal guardian before submitting personal information.

Related policies and contact

This notice supplements the sitewide Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. Questions and requests can be sent to hello@christiangoblin.com.
