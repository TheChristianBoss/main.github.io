Privacy & Data

Plain-language notes about how Christian Goblin tools and pages handle information.

Browser-based tools

Many Christian Goblin tools are designed to work in your browser. File conversion, resume drafting, cover letter drafting, and similar tools may store temporary drafts or settings in your browser storage so your work is not lost when you refresh.

For these browser tools, keep your own backups. Clearing browser data may erase saved drafts.

Files you use with tools

When a tool can process a file locally, the goal is to keep that file on your device. Some heavy or specialized features may load helper code from the site or supported libraries, but the site should not need your files just to run normal browser conversions.

Do not use the tools as your only copy of an important file. Always keep originals.

Store, services, and contact forms

When you purchase a product or service, payment and checkout details are handled through the checkout provider. Service request details you submit may be sent to Christian Goblin so the request can be fulfilled and you can be contacted.

The cybersecurity contact form collects the name, email, and message you enter so the request can be answered. Do not place passwords, sensitive account credentials, government IDs, or private medical/legal information inside service notes or contact forms.

External links

Christian Goblin may link to Discord, Stripe checkout, social pages, or other external sites. Those sites have their own privacy practices and account systems.

Contact

Questions or concerns can be raised through the community links or the contact method currently provided on the site.
