Converter Safety

Privacy & Limits

The file converter is designed as a browser-first tool. Most conversions happen on your device instead of being uploaded to Christian Goblin.

Privacy

For the current static converter, files are read by your browser and processed locally. The site does not intentionally upload your files to Christian Goblin during normal conversions.

Heavy audio/video helpers may load conversion code from a public JavaScript CDN when the media module is opened. Your selected file still runs in the browser for that module.

Limitations

  • Large video, PDF, ZIP, DOCX, or XLSX files can use much more memory than their original file size.
  • Password-protected, encrypted, damaged, or unusual files may not convert.
  • Mobile devices and low-memory laptops may need smaller files.
  • Always keep the original file until you confirm the converted output looks right.

Safe mode

Image, text, JSON, CSV, Base64, hashing, and many simple PDF/ZIP actions are the safest everyday browser conversions.

Heavy mode

Audio/video conversion, very large PDFs, and large spreadsheet jobs may be slow. The app shows device-aware file-size guidance before those tasks.
